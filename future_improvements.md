# Future Improvements - Infestium

## Global Infestation Mechanics
1. **Dynamic World Simulation**: Real-time population dynamics with cities growing, falling, and recovering from infestations
2. **Government Response Systems**: Adaptive AI for military, medical, and political responses to infestation spread
3. **Economic Impact Modeling**: Market crashes, supply chain disruptions, and resource scarcity affecting strategy
4. **Media & Information Warfare**: Control public perception through misinformation, propaganda, and social media manipulation
5. **International Relations**: Diplomatic tensions, border closures, and alliance formations in response to threats

## Agent Evolution & Customization
6. **Genetic Engineering Interface**: Visual DNA editor for creating custom agent strains with balanced risk-reward mechanics
7. **Symbiotic Relationships**: Agents working together with host organisms for mutual benefit and stealth
8. **Environmental Adaptation**: Specialized variants for different climates, urban environments, and defensive measures
9. **Technological Integration**: Bio-mechanical hybrids incorporating human technology for enhanced capabilities
10. **Hive Mind Coordination**: Advanced collective intelligence systems with emergent behavior patterns

## Strategic Gameplay Depth
11. **Multiple Victory Conditions**: Conquest, assimilation, symbiosis, or transcendence paths with unique requirements
12. **Counter-Agent Development**: Humanity's adaptive responses creating new challenges and strategic considerations
13. **Time Management**: Multi-generational campaigns with long-term planning and evolutionary pressures
14. **Resource Management**: Biomass, energy, and genetic material as strategic resources requiring careful allocation
15. **Stealth Operations**: Covert infiltration mechanics avoiding detection while building infestation networks

## Scientific Realism Features
16. **Epidemiological Modeling**: Realistic disease spread patterns based on actual scientific research
17. **Genetic Mutation Systems**: Authentic DNA modification mechanics with realistic biological constraints
18. **Ecological Interactions**: Complex food webs and environmental impacts of infestation activities
19. **Research & Development**: Human scientific progress creating new countermeasures and detection methods
20. **Ethical Dilemma Integration**: Moral choice systems exploring the philosophical implications of species evolution
